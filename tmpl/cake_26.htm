在流星飞过的时候许一个愿望，和ta一起许下爱的诺言。

学着古希腊人的样子，
闭眼许愿。
即使错过了今晚的流星，
依然可以和你一起许下爱的诺言。

Make a promise of love
Like the ancient Greeks once did.
Make a wish, with eyes closed. But never blindly.
Trace a shooting star with your finger,
You may not catch it. Don't ever stop trying.
